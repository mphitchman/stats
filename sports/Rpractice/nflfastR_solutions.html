Setup

  • Create a new script.
  • Record (as comment) your name in line 1.
  • Load the tidyverse and nflfastR packages:
library(tidyverse)
library(nflfastR)
library(kableExtra) # I also use kableExtra to generate nice tables

Then load play-by-play data for the 2024 regular season:

df <- load_pbp(seasons = 2024) |> 
  filter(season_type == 'REG')

7. How many times did a team go for it on fourth down and 4? How often did such a team run the ball? How often did they throw it? How often were teams successful?

A table can answer the firsts question:

fourth_and_4 <- df |> filter(down==4 & ydstogo == 4)

table(fourth_and_4$play_type)
## 
## field_goal    no_play       pass       punt        run 
##         96         25         47        148          9

A two-way table can answer the next two:

table(fourth_and_4$play_type,
      fourth_and_4$first_down
      )
##             
##                0   1
##   field_goal  96   0
##   no_play     18   7
##   pass        27  20
##   punt       148   0
##   run          1   8

By inspection, a run play was successful in 8 of the 9 attempts (88.9% of the time - small sample size), and pass plays were successful in 20 of the 47 attempts (42.5% of the time).

9. Which teams had the worst/best field goal percentage for attempts under 40 yards?

field_goals<-df |>
  filter(play_type=="field_goal",season_type=="REG")|>
  select(game_date,time,down,yrdln,ydstogo,yardline_100,posteam,
         defteam,field_goal_result,field_goal_attempt,
         game_seconds_remaining,ep,contains("prob"),game_id)|>
  mutate(success=ifelse(field_goal_result=="made",1,0),
         fg_distance=yardline_100+17)

short_fg_by_team <- field_goals |> 
  filter(fg_distance < 40) |>
  group_by(posteam) |> 
  summarize(attempts = n(),
            made = sum(success)) |>
  mutate(short_fg_pct = made/attempts)
